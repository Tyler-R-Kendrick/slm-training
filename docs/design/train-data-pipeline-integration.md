reserved-structure-filtered recipe lacked `meta.contract_id`. The integrated

provide one. The writer now retains the authoritative stamp generated by F1
while continuing to avoid inventing legacy lineage or family metadata.

root, one RICO screen, `--allow-missing-design-md`, and the reserved test

`9359d47176c0ec75fce9389558af69884a6ce4afc47f89fc0f7412987ad4bf17`;
all 176 rows carried the pinned contract ID. The focused train-data harness
